# Privacy Notice

**Last updated:** April 7, 2026

## 1. Data Controller

The owner of ThreatWarden ("the Bot") is the data controller responsible for the processing of your data. For contact details, see Section 13 below.

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary for the Bot to function:

- **Discord User ID** — to identify who submitted files and respond with results
- **Submitted Files** — temporarily, for the duration of analysis
- **File Hashes** — (MD5, SHA-256) for identification and deduplication
- **Scan Results** — detection metadata, analysis timestamps
- **Error Logs** — technical logs for debugging purposes

## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal information beyond your Discord User ID
- Message content other than files you submit
- Voice, image, or location data
- Any data from sources outside Discord interactions with the Bot

## 4. Legal Basis

Processing is based on our **legitimate interest** (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in providing the Bot's functionality, maintaining service integrity, and improving detection capabilities. You have the right to object to this processing at any time under Art. 21 GDPR by contacting us (see Section 13). Where objection is justified, we will cease processing for those purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Files:** Deleted from the Bot's processing environment after analysis is complete. However, files you submit remain visible in the Discord channel/thread where they were uploaded and are subject to Discord's own data retention policies.
- **Retention for analysis:** In individual cases, files may be retained temporarily for further manual analysis (e.g. investigating new malware samples or false positives/negatives).
- **Hashes & Results:** Retained only as long as necessary for the Bot's operation and improving detection accuracy.
- **Logs:** Retained for debugging and operational purposes, including in dedicated Discord logging channels.
- No data is sold, traded, or shared with third parties.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Scan metadata (hashes, results) may be retained internally for improving detection capabilities. Submitted files are not redistributed by us, though they remain visible in the Discord channel/thread where uploaded.

## 7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

- **Access** — Request information about data we store about you
- **Rectification** — Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction** — Request limitation of data processing
- **Portability** — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection** — Object to data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, contact the Bot administrators via Discord.

## 8. International Transfers

Data processing occurs on the servers hosting the Bot. If you are located outside the European Economic Area (EEA), be aware that your data may be transferred to servers in other jurisdictions with different data protection standards.

## 9. Discord Data

Discord's own [Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) apply to your use of Discord. We do not control data collected by Discord itself.

## 10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## 11. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us for removal.

## 12. Changes

This Privacy Notice may be updated at any time. Users will be notified of material changes where feasible. The updated version will be available at the same location with a new "Last updated" date.

## 13. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the Bot administrators via Discord.
